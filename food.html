Traditional Food

Soto Betawi

Soto Betawi is a hearty soup that originates from Jakarta. It features tender beef or offal pieces simmered in a rich and aromatic coconut milk broth, seasoned with herbs and spices like lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves.

Ketoprak

Ketoprak is a popular Jakarta street food dish made with tofu, rice vermicelli, and rice cakes, all smothered in a peanut sauce. It's typically garnished with bean sprouts, fried shallots, and sometimes boiled eggs. Ketoprak is known for its savory and slightly sweet flavors.

Kerak Telor

Kerak Telor is a savory rice cake made from glutinous rice and duck or chicken eggs, cooked with a mixture of coconut, fried shallots, dried shrimp, and other seasonings. The mixture is spread thinly on a hot griddle until it becomes crispy and golden brown.
